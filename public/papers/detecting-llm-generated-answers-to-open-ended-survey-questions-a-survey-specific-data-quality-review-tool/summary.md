# Detecting LLM-Generated Answers to Open-Ended Survey Questions: A Survey-Specific Data-Quality Review Tool

Status: under-review
Visibility: metadata-only
Status label: Submitted to Public Opinion Quarterly
Authors: with Brandon Sepulvado, Lilian Huang, and Ting Yan

## Summary

This research note develops and validates a survey-specific detector for AI-like answers to open-ended survey questions. The detector is framed as a triage signal within layered data-quality review rather than a standalone basis for fraud classification or respondent exclusion.
